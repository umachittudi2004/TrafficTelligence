About the Project

TrafficTelligence uses machine learning to predict traffic volume with precision, leveraging data like weather, events, and historical traffic. It enables smarter traffic control, informed urban planning, and better commuter routing.

Key Use Cases

Dynamic Traffic Management

Authorities can use real-time predictions to adjust signal timings and optimize lanes for smoother traffic flow.

Urban Planning

Helps city planners design road networks and public transport systems based on predicted future traffic loads.

Commuter Guidance

Navigation apps and users can plan routes better by avoiding congested areas and travel during optimal times.
